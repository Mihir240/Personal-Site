Dice Game

Written in: Javascript, Html, Css

View
Info

Objective: This a 2-player dice game with the intent of one player obtaining 21 points first!

Rules:

  1. Each player can roll a dice as many times as they wish on their turn!
  2. If a player rolls a "1" on their turn, they lose all their points!
  3. Alternatively, a player can "hold" their points if they find that rolling a dice is too risky! However, holding will give your opponent a turn!
